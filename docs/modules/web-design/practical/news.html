Popular Movie Star Sheila Najeeb Scares Off Potential Home Burglar

Sheila's home alarm system came through in a big way by alerting her that a potential thief was lurking outside, and Sheila was able to scare the perp off.

The female actress was at her home in the Capital City Thursday night when somebody smashed one of her back windows and triggered her alarm ... according to law enforcement sources. We're told Sheila was quick to act and turn on the lights ... causing the suspect or suspects to take off.

Sheila made a 119 call and cops arrived shortly after and other than the broken window ... we're told everything at her home's shipshape.

As we've reported ... a crime wave of celeb burglaries has hit the Capital the past several months, but fortunately for Sheila the thieves left empty-handed this time.
